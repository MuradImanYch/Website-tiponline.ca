We believe that tiponline is good for all kinds of services. Avoid hidden fees, get your tips directly to your account.

How to connect

Connect with API

API connection for business with CRM technology, you will be able to send text with a link to your customer for online tipping. Good to companies with call centers and back offices. Each staff member will have their own account.

Scan QR code

Create your own QR code, print it or save it on your smartphone. Good for all types of business specially for self-employed and small businesses (mechanicals, waiters, chefs, guides, barbers etc.)

Button or widget

Put tiponline.ca widget on your webpage and start to receive online tips. Good for all websites especially for bloggers, vloggers etc.
